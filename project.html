    Bombom Relligy Shop E-Commerce Website

    Bombom Religy Shop is simple e-commerce website that I developed with a friend of mine as our final project for Technopreneurship subject in my university. The concept of this project appeared due to the lack of relligy shop in Indonesia that sell products from all religion in Indonesia. Most of the time, a relligy shop only provide / sell product for a single religion. My team wanted to make a business that sell religion product for every religion in Indonesia.

    Foodictive Capstone Project

    Foodictive is an app that intends to help people, especially tourists to identify their food. User only need to upload or scan a photo of their food and then our app will show every detailed information about that food such as description, recipe, and location of food seller around them. This app can also helps local food vendor in Indonesia. Because, tourist can find every local food vendor using this app. I developed this app with my team as our capstone project in Bangkit Academy 2022.
