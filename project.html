The Maze Game Development

The Maze is a game that I developed during my 5th semester using Unity. This game can be played in Android and Windows platform. In this game, player needs to move their "character" through the maze and reach the star at the end of the maze. But player should be aware of the enemies that patrolled in this maze. There are 2 potions in this game, red and blue potion. Blue potion will regen player's HP. Meanwhile the red one will reduce player's HP. These are the features that currently available on this game.

  • Support Multi-Platform (Android & Windows)
  • Lightweight
  • Multiplayer (2 players)
  • User Friendly Interface
  • Available with 3 varian level

    Bombom Relligy Shop E-Commerce Website

    Bombom Religy Shop is a simple e-commerce website that I developed with a group of my friends as our final project for Technopreneurship subject in my university. The concept of this project appeared due to the lack of relligy shop in Indonesia that sell products from all religion in Indonesia. Most of the time, a relligy shop only provide/sell product for a single religion. My team wanted to make a business that sell religion product for every religion in Indonesia. We made a business plan together that can be seen at this link. Our business plan was calculated and planned to be an offline shop. In addition to that, we also provide online service using this website. So people can buy our products at ease.

    Foodictive Capstone Project

    Foodictive is an app that intends to help people, especially tourists to identify their food. User only need to upload or scan a photo of their food and then our app will show every detailed information about that food such as description, recipe, and location of food vendor around them. By showing the location of every local food vendor around user, we also hope that this app could support local food vendor in Indonesia. I developed this app with my team as our capstone project in Bangkit Academy 2022. This project is saved in my team's GitHub
